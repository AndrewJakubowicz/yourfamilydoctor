Knoxfield & Colchester Medical Centres and Travel Doctor
living healthier, home and abroad

At Knoxfield and Colchester Medical Centres our doctors are committed to providing the best care for you and your family.

We aim to provide a comprehensive range of services that are not found in smaller practices and use the most up to date medical equipment and sterilizing techniques.

We are also Government Authorised Travel Vaccination Centres offering a full travel medicine service.

We stock all vaccines and a range of travel related products.
